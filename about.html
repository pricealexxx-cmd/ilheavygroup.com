About IL Heavy Group

Your trusted partner in premium construction equipment

Who We Are

IL Heavy Group LLC is a premier provider of high-quality Bobcat compact track loaders and construction equipment. Based in Lisle, Illinois, we serve construction professionals, contractors, and businesses throughout the region with top-tier equipment solutions.

Founded with a commitment to excellence, we specialize in connecting our clients with the right equipment for their specific needs. Whether you're working on a small residential project or a large commercial development, we have the expertise and inventory to support your success.

Our Mission

At IL Heavy Group, our mission is simple: to provide construction professionals with access to premium Bobcat equipment backed by exceptional customer service and industry expertise.

We believe in building lasting relationships with our clients, understanding their unique requirements, and delivering solutions that enhance productivity and profitability on the job site.

Our Equipment Range

Premium Bobcat compact track loaders for every application

We specialize in Bobcat compact track loaders, offering a carefully selected range of models to suit different project requirements:

  • Bobcat T590: Perfect balance of power and versatility for medium-duty applications
